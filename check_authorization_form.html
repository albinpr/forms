üîí INTERNAL CONTROL PROCEDURES

  • Segregation of Duties: The preparer cannot be one of the approvers. At least two independent reviewers must authorize payment.
  • Documentation Requirements: All payments must be supported by original invoices and supporting documentation attached to this form.
  • Dual Authorization: Both reviewer signatures are required before check release. Verbal approvals are not acceptable.
  • Budget Verification: Reviewer #1 must confirm adequate budget availability before approval.
  • Authority Limits: Payments exceeding $10,000 require CFO/Controller signature as Reviewer #2.
  • Retention: This form and all supporting documents must be retained for 7 years per company policy.
  • Review Frequency: All check authorization forms are subject to quarterly internal audit review.
